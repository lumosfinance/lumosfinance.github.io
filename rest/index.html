The Realm of Lumos

01Dare to learn new spells?

If your answer is yes, feel free to join our world and encounter the adventures waiting for you. You can start learning new spells, which would be very helpful in your journey ahead. Learning spells not only gets you an NFT diploma at the end of the education period, but you will also be able to craft the critical ingredient $LMS.

$LMS will be your fuel to learn new spells in the NFT Crafting period. NFT spells are far more advanced than the elemental magic in the yield crafting period. So, the more $LMS you have, the more NFT spells you will learn in the second semester.

02$LMS Sale at Bounce Finance Market (Bazaar)

A certain amount of $LMS needs to be created before the yield crafting period begins. That’s necessary for two reasons:
  1. The Realm of Lumos becomes unstable when all the $LMS diminishes. So to keep the balance, $LMS needs to be created.
  2. It’s the critical ingredient to cast our spells. Without $LMS, adventurers can’t start learning new spells.
Elderly wizards will once more trigger the ancient magic to create $LMS after all this time. Then they will be sold in Bounce Finance Bazaar. 480.000 $LMS will be created in this phase and 300.000 of them will be sold in Bounce Finance. The exchange ratio for 1 $LMS will be 0.005 ETH in this sale. All the information and links regarding the sale will be shared on our official channels. After the Bounce sale is over, %33 of the ETH gathered in the sale will be locked in Uniswap Bazaar with 80.000 $LMS. However, the Uniswap listing price will be %25 more expensive than the Bounce Sale price.

Wizard Treasury

Wizard Crafting
(20.000 - 1%)

Wizards help you with your spelling classes. Every time you put effort into a spell, you craft some LMS. It depends on your ingredient size and your effort. The wizards helping you on these spells also craft some LMS, though not as much as you do. Less than %1 of the total supply.

Yield Crafting
(1.500.000 - 75%)

The most significant share will be allocated to the students as a reward for the hard work while learning how to craft in our pools. They will be able to craft up to 1.508.698 LMS in the yield crafting period.

04Roadmap

Presale
Elderly wizards are working on the magic to craft some $LMS to be sold on the Bounce bazaar. The sale will take place in the third week of October.



Uniswap Listing

Uniswap listing will be done immediately after the presale. %33 of the gathered ETH will be used and the liquidity will be locked for six months.



Yield Crafting

Upon Uniswap listing, apprentices will be able to learn casting new spells. They will craft $LMS during the education period. Yield crafting will continue until the total amount of $LMS reaches 2.000.000.



NFT Diplomas

Students who successfully crafted $LMS will be eligible to receive their own diploma amongst the limited number of NFT diplomas for a certain period. These will be used later in their journey.



NFT crafting

Upon yield crafting, you will learn the elemental spells and it will be time to become a real sorcerer. You will be able to learn new spells or you can acquire new heroes to your team by participating in NFT crafting. That’s where you will gain your real significant power.



Lumos Challenges

After you acquire your NFT power-ups, spells and skills, you will be ready to choose your way. Be a stand-alone hero, challenge others for artifacts or declare your loyalty to a renowned king. A whole new journey awaits...
